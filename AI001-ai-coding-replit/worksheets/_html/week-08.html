🤖 AI001 Week 8 — English Worksheet

Topic: Final Project — Your Own App · Course: AI Coding with Replit Agent · Time: about 45 minutes (plus build time)

This is the big one. You will combine every skill from the past 7 weeks — specific prompts, context and examples, step-by-step building, iteration, debugging, and understanding code — to build your own app from start to finish. The idea must be yours.

Your project must include:

  • an idea that comes from you,
  • at least 5 prompt steps to build it,
  • at least 3 iteration prompts to improve it,
  • at least 1 debugging moment,
  • a note explaining what the key parts of your code do.

1 · Plan Your Idea 🔮

3 · Show Your Finished App 📸

When your app works, send the public link to it, or a video of it running. Then explain your project using these sentence starters — write 5 to 7 sentences total.

I built an app that …

I chose this idea because …

I split my idea into … steps.

The hardest problem was … and I solved it by …

The trickiest part of the code is …

One thing I am proud of is …

If I built it without AI, I would have to start by …

4 · Final Presentation 🎥

Record a short video (your phone, or a parent's phone) presenting your app, as if showing it to the class. Try to use these words: prompt, step, iterate, debug, understand.

  1. Show your finished app running.
  2. Read your one-sentence idea and show how you split it into steps.
  3. Show one part of the code and explain what it does in your own words.
  4. Tell the biggest problem you hit and how you solved it.

Write what you will say in your video. Plan it here before you record — you can read from it while filming.
