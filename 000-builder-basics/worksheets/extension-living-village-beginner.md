# 🏰 Builder Basics — The Living Village (Extension · Beginner)

**Topic:** Bring It All Together · **Course:** Builder Basics · **Level:** Beginner · **Type:** Extension Activity · **Time:** about 40 minutes

> 🧩 A bonus challenge, not a weekly lesson. Try it after **Complete Your Village**.

---

## 1 · The Challenge 🎯

Make your village feel **alive** — with crops, animals, and water. It must have **all eight** of these:

> 🌾 Farm
> 🏠 Farm house
> 🗼 House with a watchtower
> 🧱 Fence
> 💧 Pond with fish
> 🌉 Bridge over the pond
> 🪣 Well
> 🐄 A few animals

**One rule: build with… Circle one:** code · your hands

**Tick each one when it is done in your world:**

> ☐ 🌾 Farm
> ☐ 🏠 Farm house
> ☐ 🗼 House with a watchtower
> ☐ 🧱 Fence
> ☐ 💧 Pond with fish
> ☐ 🌉 Bridge over the pond
> ☐ 🪣 Well
> ☐ 🐄 A few animals

---

## 2 · Match It 🔗

**A farm is made of rows of… Circle one:** crops · doors · lava

**A watchtower is a tower that is… Circle one:** tall · flat · underground

**A bridge goes over the… Circle one:** pond · roof · farm

**Fish live in the… Circle one:** pond · fence · well

---

## 3 · Use a Loop 🔁

A loop repeats the same step again and again.

**Which one is best made with a loop? Circle one:** one animal · a row of crops · one block

**A fence has four sides. Between each side, the agent must… Circle one:** turn · stop · fly away

---

## 4 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Go to your world. Build them with code: the **farm**, **farm house**, **watchtower house**, **fence**, **pond with fish**, **bridge**, and **well**. Then add a **few animals**.

Record **one video** (a phone is fine). Show two things:

**1 · Your code.** Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

**2 · Your build.** Point the camera. Name the parts.

Say these out loud, filling in the blanks:

> Today I built ______.
>
> I built it using this code: ______.
>
> In this code I used ______.
>
> The hardest part was ______.
>
> That part was hard because ______.
