# 🎮 M000 Pre-Lesson — Get Comfy in Minecraft

**Topic:** Moving & Building · **Course:** Builder Basics · **Time:** about 30 minutes

---

This is a warm-up **before Lesson 1**. No coding yet. The goal is to get comfortable playing Minecraft — moving around and placing blocks — so Lesson 1 feels easy. Open your Minecraft world and have fun.

---

## 1 · Move Around 🎮

Get into your world and try each move. Tick the box when you can do it.

- ☐ Walk forward, back, left, and right
- ☐ Look around by moving the mouse
- ☐ Jump
- ☐ Look up at the sky, then down at the ground
- ☐ Walk up onto a hill or a block

> On a computer: walk with **W A S D**, look with the **mouse**, jump with the **space bar**.

**Which move was the trickiest? Write one sentence.**

---

## 2 · Break and Place Blocks 🧱

Now change the world a little. Tick each one.

- ☐ Break one block (dig it away)
- ☐ Place one block
- ☐ Open your inventory and pick a new kind of block
- ☐ Build a small tower 3 blocks tall
- ☐ Break your tower back down

**Which block did you like the most? Write one sentence.**

<div class="write-space short"></div>

---

## 3 · Build a House 🏠

Now build a small house by hand. Any size is fine — it does not need to be perfect. Your house needs **4 things**:

- ☐ Four walls
- ☐ A door you can walk through
- ☐ Two windows (use glass, or leave two holes)
- ☐ A roof on top

Take your time and try different blocks.

---

## 4 · Tell Me About Your House 📸

Send a photo of your finished house to your teacher. Then finish these sentences — write 3 or 4.

> My house is made of …
>
> The door is on the …
>
> My favourite part is …
>
> The hardest part was …

<div class="write-space tall" style="min-height: 300px"></div>

---

## 5 · Show Me Around 🎥

Take a short video walking around your house. Talk like you are showing a friend. Try to use these words: **walls**, **door**, **window**, **roof**.

> 1. Walk up to your house.
> 2. Show the door and walk inside.
> 3. Point to the windows and the roof.

**Write what you will say in your video.** You can read from it while filming.
