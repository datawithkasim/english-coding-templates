# 🎮 M000 Pre-Lesson — Get Comfy in Minecraft

**Topic:** Moving & Building · **Course:** Builder Basics · **Time:** about 15 minutes

---

A quick warm-up **before Lesson 1**. No coding. Just get comfortable moving around and building on your tablet. Open your Minecraft world and play.

---

## 1 · Move Around 🎮

Try each one. Tick the box when you can do it.

- ☐ Walk
- ☐ Look around
- ☐ Jump
- ☐ Tap to place a block
- ☐ Hold to break a block

> On a tablet: move with the **left thumb pad**, look by **dragging the right side**, **tap** to place a block, **hold** to break one.

---

## 2 · Build a House 🏠

Build a small house. Any size is fine. It needs **3 things**:

- ☐ Walls (use any block)
- ☐ A door
- ☐ A window (use a **glass pane**)

---

## 3 · Send It 📸

Take a photo of your house and send it to your teacher on KakaoTalk.
