🏘️ Builder Basics — Complete Your Village (Extension)

Topic: Bring It All Together · Course: Builder Basics · Type: Extension Activity · Time: about 60 minutes

🧩 This is a bonus challenge, not a weekly lesson. Try it after the Week 8 village project, once you can build with loops and move the agent between spots.

1 · The Challenge 🎯

Your village has a good start, but a real village needs more than walls. Complete your village so it feels like a place someone could live.

By the end, your village must have all four of these:

🏠 A house with a roof 🪣 A well 💧 A small pond 🌸 A small flower bed

There is no fixed size and no map — you decide how big each thing is and where it goes. One rule you must not break:

⚠️ Everything must be built with code. No placing blocks by hand.

What four things must your village have when you finish?

Why do you think we say "build it with code" instead of placing blocks by hand?

2 · Plan Before You Build ✏️

A builder plans first. For each of the four structures, decide the blocks, the size, and whether you need a loop.

House with a roof — What blocks? How wide and how tall? Where does the roof sit?

Well — What shape is the hole? What goes around the top? Is there water inside?

Small pond — How will you make the dip in the ground? What fills it?

Small flower bed — What holds the soil? How many flowers, and how will you place a row of them?

3 · Think in Loops 🔁

You built walls with loops before. A loop is for anything you do again and again — a row of wall blocks, a row of flowers, the four sides of a well.

Which of your four structures will use a loop, and what does the loop repeat each time?

Between one structure and the next, the agent has to walk to a new spot so nothing lands on top of the last build.

Between the house and the pond, what will the agent do to get to a clean spot?

4 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Go to your homework world and build each structure one at a time, moving the agent between them. Keep going until your village has the house with a roof, the well, the small pond, and the small flower bed — all placed by code.

Record one video (a phone is fine). Show two things:

1 · Your code. Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

2 · Your build. Point the camera. Name the parts.

Fill the blanks:

Today I built ______.

I built it using this code: ______.

In this code I used ______.

The hardest part was ______.

That part was hard because ______.

Write your lines here, then say them in your video.
