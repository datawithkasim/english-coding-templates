🏘️ Builder Basics — Complete Your Village (Extension · Beginner)

Topic: Bring It All Together · Course: Builder Basics · Level: Beginner · Type: Extension Activity · Time: about 30 minutes

🧩 A bonus challenge, not a weekly lesson. Try it after the Week 8 village.

1 · The Challenge 🎯

Finish your village. It must have all four of these:

🏠 House with a roof 🪣 Well 💧 Small pond 🌸 Small flower bed

One rule: build with… Circle one: code · your hands

Tick each one when it is done in your world:

☐ 🏠 House with a roof ☐ 🪣 Well ☐ 💧 Small pond ☐ 🌸 Small flower bed

2 · Match It 🔗

A pond is made of a dip in the ground filled with… Circle one: water · lava · flowers

A flower bed is a row of… Circle one: flowers · doors · stairs

A house needs a roof so it has a… Circle one: top · hole · door

3 · Use a Loop 🔁

A loop repeats the same step again and again.

Which one is best made with a loop? Circle one: one door · a row of flowers · one block

Between the house and the pond, the agent must first… Circle one: walk to a new spot · stop · turn off

4 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Go to your world. Build all four with code: the house with a roof, the well, the small pond, and the small flower bed.

Record one video (a phone is fine). Show two things:

1 · Your code. Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

2 · Your build. Point the camera. Name the parts.

Fill the blanks:

Today I built ______.

I built it using this code: ______.

In this code I used ______.

The hardest part was ______.

That part was hard because ______.

Write your lines here, then say them in your video.
