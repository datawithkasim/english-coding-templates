## 1 · ON and OFF 👀

The agent walks. A switch is **ON** or **OFF**.

| Switch | The agent drops… |
|:------:|:----------------:|
| **ON** ✅ | 🟪 🟪 🟪 🟪 blocks |
| **OFF** ❌ | nothing (empty) |

**ON = blocks.  OFF = no blocks.**
