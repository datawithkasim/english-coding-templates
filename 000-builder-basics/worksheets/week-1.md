## 3 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Now switch to your homework world. Move your agent in an **L-shape** path — go forward, turn, then go forward again (no loops this week). When you finish, come back here.

Send a photo or video of where the agent ended up, then explain what you did. Use these sentence starters — write 4 to 6 sentences total.

> First, I told the agent to …
>
> Then I made it turn …
>
> The hardest part was …
>
> To fix it, I …
>
> The agent ended up at …
>
> If I had more time, I would …

## 4 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

Now take a video on your phone (or a parent's phone) while you walk the camera through your world. Talk through it like you are teaching someone who has never seen it. Try to use these words: **forward**, **turn**, **up**, **down**, **agent**.

> 1. Show where the agent started.
> 2. Read your commands out loud one by one and say what each one does.
> 3. Show one bug you hit and how you fixed it.
> 4. Show where the agent ended up.
