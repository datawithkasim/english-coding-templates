# 🎮 RB005 Week 2 — English Worksheet

**Topic:** Greybox It — Find the Fun First · **Course:** Dream Game Studio · **Time:** about 45 minutes

This week you build your WHOLE map with plain gray parts. No colors, no decorations, no fancy models. This is called **greyboxing** — it lets you find the fun fast, before you spend hours making things pretty.

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read each situation. Write what you think before class.

**A student spends the whole first week decorating a beautiful start area. In week 3 they finally playtest — and the game is boring. What went wrong with their order of work?**

<div class="write-space"></div>

**Your greybox map is ugly, but you can already play it from start to goal in 2 minutes. Why is this MORE useful right now than a beautiful map you cannot play yet?**

<div class="write-space"></div>

**During a playtest you find a jump that is too easy, so a whole section is boring. Your parts are plain gray boxes. How hard is it to move them around? What if that section were already fully decorated?**

<div class="write-space"></div>

## 2 · Spot the Problem 🐛

Each layout plan below is broken. Read what the plan is **supposed** to do, then explain what is wrong and how you would fix it.

**Plan A** — The player should play from start to finish.

```
START platform → big open field → ???
```

**The player spawns, runs around the field … and then what? What is missing? Where would you put it?**

<div class="write-space"></div>

**Plan B** — The game should take a few minutes and feel like a challenge.

```
START platform → GOAL flag (3 steps away)
```

**A player wins in 3 seconds without doing anything. What is missing in the middle? What would you add?**

<div class="write-space"></div>

## 3 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Now open Roblox Studio and greybox your WHOLE layout from your week 1 design — start, path, goal — all in plain gray parts. Then playtest it for 10 minutes. When you finish, come back here.

Send a photo or video of your greybox map, then explain what you found. Use these sentence starters — write 4 to 6 sentences total.

> My greybox map has …
>
> The player starts at … and wins by …
>
> In my playtest, the fun part was …
>
> The boring part was …
>
> After playtesting, I changed … because …
>
> Before I decorate, I still want to fix …

<div class="write-space tall" style="min-height: 340px"></div>

---

## 4 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

Now take a video on your phone (or a parent's phone) while you walk through your greybox map in Studio. Narrate the player's path like a tour guide. Try to use these words: **greybox**, **layout**, **start**, **goal**, **playtest**.

> 1. Start at the spawn and walk the path a player would take.
> 2. Point at the goal and say the win condition.
> 3. Show the most fun part and say why it is fun.
> 4. Show one thing you changed after your playtest.
