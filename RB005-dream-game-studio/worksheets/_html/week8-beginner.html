Topic: Launch & Showcase — Publish and Present · Course: Dream Game Studio · Level: Beginner · Time: about 30 minutes

This is it — launch week! You publish your game as Public and present it like a real game studio.

1 · Predict 🔮

Read the situation. Before it happens for real, circle or write your answer.

Your game is published and set to Public. Your classmate searches its title on Roblox from their own house.

Can your classmate find and play your game? Circle one: yes · no

Why?

Your game is still set to Private.

Can a stranger play it? Circle one: yes · no

2 · Find the Difference 🐛

Each pair shows the clean launch setup first, then a broken version of the same idea. Circle what's different and write one short sentence about the problem.

Pair A — Everyone in class should be able to join your game today.

-- clean
Game Settings → Permissions → Public

-- buggy
Game Settings → Permissions → Private

What is wrong? What happens when your classmates try to join?

Pair B — Players should start with 0 coins and earn them by playing.

-- clean
local coins = Instance.new("IntValue")
coins.Name = "Coins"
coins.Value = 0

-- buggy
local coins = Instance.new("IntValue")
coins.Name = "Coins"
coins.Value = 999

What is wrong? Why is starting with 999 coins a problem on launch day?

4 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Publish your game as Public, then plan your showcase here. Send a photo or video of your live game page, then write your plan. Use these sentence starters — write 2 or 3 sentences.

My game is called … and you play it by …

5 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

This week your video IS the presentation! Take a video on your phone (or a parent's phone) and present your game like a real game studio. Try to use these words: publish, public, bug, script.

  1. Say your game's name and show the title screen.
  2. Play your game on camera and show how to win.
  3. Tell your hardest bug story.
  4. Say what you would build next.

Write what you will say in your video. You can read from it while filming.
