# 🎮 RB005 Week 7 — English Worksheet (Beginner)

**Topic:** Polish & Store Page — Ready for Strangers · **Course:** Dream Game Studio · **Level:** Beginner · **Time:** about 30 minutes

This week your game gets its **store page**: a title, a description, and an icon picture. The big question: **could a stranger play your game without your help?**

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read the store page. Before you imagine a real player seeing it, circle or write your answer.

> **Title:** FREE ROBUX OBBY!!!
> **The game:** a coin maze. There is no free Robux.

**A player joins because of this title. Will they be happy with the game? Circle one:** yes · no

**Why?**

## 2 · Find the Difference 🐛

Each pair shows a clean store page first, then a broken version of the same idea. Circle what's different and write one short sentence about the problem.

**Pair A** — The title should be short, honest, and exciting.

```
-- clean
Lava Tower: Jump to the Top!
```

```
-- buggy
💯FREE ROBUX BEST GAME EVER 100% REAL💯
```

**What is wrong with the buggy title?**

<div class="write-space short"></div>

**Pair B** — The description should tell a stranger what to do.

```
-- clean
Collect 8 keys to open the exit door. Watch out for the lava bridge!
```

```
-- buggy
good game pls play
```

**What is wrong? What does the buggy description forget to tell players?**

<div class="write-space short"></div>

## 4 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Now polish your own game's store page. Write a title, write a short description, and take a nice icon screenshot (in the game, not in Studio's editor windows). When you finish, come back here.

Send a photo or video of your store page, then explain what you did. Use these sentence starters — write 2 or 3 sentences.

> My game's title is …
>
> My description tells players …
>
> For my icon, I took a picture of …

<div class="write-space tall" style="min-height: 240px"></div>

---

## 5 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

Take a video on your phone (or a parent's phone) — a short **trailer** for your game. Talk like an advert that makes a friend want to play. Try to use these words: **title**, **goal**, **win**, **play**.

> 1. Say your game's title and what kind of game it is.
> 2. Show the coolest part of your game.
> 3. Say what players must do to win.

**Write what you will say in your video.** You can read from it while filming.
