🧩 M002 Extension 2 — Piston & Logic Maze

Topic: Two Pistons + AND · Course: Maze Madness · Level: Extension (Beginner) · Time: about 30 minutes

The agent walks a redstone trail and flips a lever to open a piston gate. Get it to the end.

1 · Chat Words 🎛️

A word makes the agent move.

type "r":
    turn right

You type r. Circle one: turn left · turn right · jump

type "rl":
    come back to you

You type rl. Circle one: to the goal · back to you · nowhere

2 · AND means BOTH 🔍

AND is true only when both are true.

if redstone left AND redstone ahead:
    turn left

Redstone is left, but NOT ahead. Turn left? Circle one: yes · no

Redstone is left AND ahead. Turn left? Circle one: yes · no

3 · Fill the Gap ✏️

Turn left only when both are true.

if redstone left ____ redstone ahead:
    turn left

Missing word? Circle one: AND · OR
