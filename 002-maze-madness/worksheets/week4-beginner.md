# 🔀 M002 Week 4 — AND / OR

**Topic:** AND and OR · **Course:** Maze Madness · **Level:** Beginner · **Time:** about 25 minutes

**AND** = both true. **OR** = one is enough.

---

## 1 · Circle 🔮

```
if wall ahead AND wall on right:
    turn left
```

**Wall ahead. Right is open. Turn? Circle one:** yes · no

```
if no wall ahead OR no wall below:
    move forward
```

**Ahead blocked. Below open. Move? Circle one:** yes · no

---

## 2 · Spot It 🐛

The agent should turn left **only** at a corner: wall ahead AND wall on right.

```
# buggy
if wall ahead OR wall on right:
    turn left
```

**It turns too often. Which word is wrong? Circle one:** OR · left · turn

---

## 3 · Fill One Blank ✏️

Move when **one** path is open.

```
if no wall ahead ____ no wall below:
    move forward
```

**Missing word? Circle one:** OR · AND · NOT
