# 🧊 M003 Week 8 — English Worksheet (Beginner)

**Topic:** Final — Two-Faced Statue · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Beginner · **Time:** about 30 minutes

This is your final build. No new commands — just everything from this course together. Your statue shows **one picture from the front** and a **different picture from the side**. The two faces meet at one corner.

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read the steps. Before you imagine it happening, circle or write your answer.

```
fill stone from (0, 0, 0) to (4, 4, 0)
```

**From the front you see a big square. From the side, do you see the same big square? Circle one:** yes · no

**Why?**

## 2 · Find the Difference 🐛

Each pair shows clean steps first, then a broken version of the same idea. Circle what's different and write one short sentence about the bug.

**Pair A** — The side face should sit at the **corner** (x = 0), not cut through the front picture.

```
# clean
fill stone from (0, 0, 0) to (4, 4, 0)
fill stone from (0, 0, 0) to (0, 4, 4)
```

```
# buggy
fill stone from (0, 0, 0) to (4, 4, 0)
fill stone from (2, 0, 0) to (2, 4, 4)
```

**What is wrong? What happens to the front picture?**

## 4 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Now switch to your homework world. Draw your **front picture** and your **side picture** on paper first, then build your two-faced statue so the faces meet at one corner. When you finish, come back here.

Send a photo or video of **both views**, then explain what you did. Use these sentence starters — write 2 or 3 sentences.

> My front picture is … and my side picture is …
>
> The two faces meet at …
>
> One tricky moment was when …

## 5 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

Take a video on your phone (or a parent's phone) while you walk around your statue. Talk like you are teaching a friend. Try to use these words: **front view**, **side view**, **face**, **height**.

> 1. Stand in front of the statue and say what the front picture shows.
> 2. Walk to the side and say what the side picture shows.
> 3. Say in your own words how the statue shows two different pictures.

**Write what you will say in your video.** You can read from it while filming.
