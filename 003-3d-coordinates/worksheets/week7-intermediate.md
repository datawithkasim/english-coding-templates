# 👾 M003 Week 7 — English Worksheet (Intermediate)

**Topic:** Recreate 2 Mob Heads (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Intermediate · **Time:** about 40 minutes

Two **mob heads** stand in your world — a **creeper** and a **skeleton**. Build the **front face first** (z stays the same), then **add depth** at z = 2 and z = 3.

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your head.

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read each set. Write a sentence about what you will see.

```
fill green block from (0, 4, 1) to (4, 8, 1)
```

**This fills a flat wall at z = 1. Is it the front face or the depth? How many blocks deep is it so far?**

## 3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Switch to your homework world. Build the **creeper head**: front face first, then depth, then the face.

```
# front face (z = 1)
fill green block from (0, 4, 1) to (4, 8, 1)

# add depth (z = 2 and z = 3)
fill green block from (0, 4, 2) to (4, 8, 3)

# face on the front (z = 1)
place black block at (1, 7, 1)
place black block at (3, 7, 1)
place black block at (2, 6, 1)
place black block at (2, 5, 1)
place black block at (1, 4, 1)
place black block at (3, 4, 1)
```

Record **one video** (a phone is fine). Show two things:

**1 · Your code.** Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

**2 · Your build.** Point the camera. Name the parts.

>
> I built it using this code: ______.
>

>
> The hardest part was ______.
>
> That part was hard because ______.
