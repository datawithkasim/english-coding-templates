# 🐼 M003 Extension — Build the Panda (Intermediate)

**Topic:** 3D Panda (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Intermediate (Extension, after Week 6) · **Time:** about 40 minutes

Build a **standing panda** in 3D — white body, black ears, black eye patches, black legs. Every block needs **three** numbers **(x, y, z)**: x **across**, y **up**, z **deeper** (forward).

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your panda.
>
> 🎨 White parts = **white concrete**. Black parts = **black wool**.

## 3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Look at the 🐼 panda in the world. Walk around it. Then build **one full panda** on your home spot — head, two ears, two eyes, two legs.

> **Front face** (keep z = 1): fill a white head from (2, 5, 1) to (5, 8, 1). Add two black ears on top — (2, 9, 1) and (5, 9, 1). Add two black eyes — (2, 7, 1) and (5, 7, 1). Add two black legs at the bottom — fill (2, 3, 1) to (2, 4, 1) and (5, 3, 1) to (5, 4, 1).
>
> **Add depth:** build the white head again at z = 2, then z = 3, until your panda is as deep as the real one. Build the ears and legs deeper too.
>
> **Face on front only:** at z = 2 and z = 3, place plain white where the eyes were.

> 💡 Tip: the eyes only show on the **front**. The sides and back are plain white.
