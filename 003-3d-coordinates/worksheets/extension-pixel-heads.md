# 🧊 M003 Extension 2 — English Worksheet

**Topic:** 3D Mob Heads (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Extension (after Week 3) · **Time:** about 45 minutes

Build **two mob heads** in 3D — a **creeper** and a **piglin**. Now every block needs **three** numbers: **(x, y, z)**. x is **across**, y is **up**, z is **forward** (depth). Both heads stand in front of you in the world — walk around them, then copy one block by block.

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your head.

## 3 · Build a Head 🧱

Pick **one** head in the world. Walk around it. Then build your own on your home spot.

**Start with the front face** (keep z the same, e.g. z = 1). Read each block's **x** (across) and **y** (up).

**Then add depth.** Build the same face again at z = 2, then z = 3, until your head is as deep as the real one.

> 💡 Tip: the eyes and mouth only show on the **front**. The sides and back are plain.

Send a photo. Then finish these — 4 to 6 sentences.

> I built the …
>
> The lowest front block is at (x = …, y = …, z = …).
>
> To go up I changed the … number; to go deeper I changed the … number.
>
> My head is … blocks deep.
>
> Once my head was wrong because …
>
> Next time I would …
