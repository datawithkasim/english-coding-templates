# 🧊 M003 Extension 2 — English Worksheet (Beginner)

**Topic:** Pixel-Art Mob Heads (x, y) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Extension (Beginner, after Week 3) · **Time:** about 30 minutes

Copy a mob head with **(x, y)**. x is **across**, y is **up**. The heads already stand in front of you in the world — look at them as you build.

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move, and the picture moves too.

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read the steps. Circle or write your answer.

```
place green block at (4, 11)
place green block at (5, 11)
place green block at (6, 11)
```

**A line across? Circle one:** across · up

**Which number stays the same — x or y?**

