# 🧊 M003 Extension 2 — English Worksheet (Beginner)

**Topic:** 3D Mob Heads (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Beginner (Extension, after Week 3) · **Time:** about 30 minutes

Build a mob head in 3D. Every block needs **three** numbers **(x, y, z)**: x **across**, y **up**, z **deeper** (forward).

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move, and the head moves too.

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read the code. Circle one word.

```
place green block at (3, 5, 1)
place green block at (3, 5, 2)
place green block at (3, 5, 3)
```

**Only z changes. Circle one:** across · up · deeper

```
place green block at (4, 5, 1)
place green block at (4, 6, 1)
place green block at (4, 7, 1)
```

**Only y changes. Circle one:** across · up · deeper

---

## 2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

This eye should be one step **deeper**, at (4, 7, 2). The code below only has **two** numbers, so it lands on the front.

```
# broken
place black block at (4, 7)
```

**Fill the blank to fix it:**

```
place black block at (4, 7, __)
```

**Which number fills the blank? Circle one:** 2 · 1

**What was missing? Circle one:** the z number · the color

---

## 3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Look at the 🟢 creeper head in the world. Walk around it.

**Build a small solid head — a cube.** Keep it easy:

> Front face: fill green blocks from (3, 5, 1) up to (5, 7, 1) — a small square.
>
> Add depth: build the same square again at z = 2, then z = 3.
>
> Add 2 eyes on the front only: black block at (3, 7, 1) and black block at (5, 7, 1).

> 💡 Tip: the eyes only show on the **front**. The sides and back are plain green.

Record **one video** (a phone is fine). Show two things:

**1 · Your code.** Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

**2 · Your build.** Point the camera. Name the parts.

Say these out loud, filling in the blanks:

> Today I built ______.
>
> I built it using this code: ______.
>
> In this code I used ______.
>
> The hardest part was ______.
>
> That part was hard because ______.
