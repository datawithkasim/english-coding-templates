# 🕷️ M003 Extension — Build the Spider (Beginner)

**Topic:** 3D Spider (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Beginner (Extension, after Week 6) · **Time:** about 30 minutes

Build a red spider in 3D — **red body**, **eight legs**, **two eyes**. Every block needs **three** numbers **(x, y, z)**: x **across**, y **up**, z **deeper** (forward).

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move, and the spider moves too.
>
> 🎨 Red parts = **red concrete**. Eyes = **black wool**.

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read the code. Circle one word.

```
place red block at (1, 1, 2)
place red block at (1, 1, 3)
place red block at (1, 1, 4)
```

**Only z changes. This leg steps... Circle one:** across · up · deeper

```
place red block at (1, 1, 2)
place red block at (5, 1, 2)
```

**Only x changes. This jumps from the left side to the right side. Circle one:** across · up · deeper

---

## 2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

An eye sits **up high on the front** of the body, at (2, 3, 3). The code below has only **two** numbers, so it drops flat on the floor.

```
# broken
place black block at (2, 3)
```

**Fill the blank to fix it:**

```
place black block at (2, 3, __)
```

**Which number fills the blank? Circle one:** 3 · 9

**What was missing? Circle one:** the z number · the color

---

## 3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Look at the 🕷️ spider in the world. Walk around it. See the fat body, the eight legs spread out, and the two eyes on the front.

**Build a small spider on your home spot.** Keep it easy:

> Red body: fill red blocks from (2, 2, 3) to (4, 3, 4) — a small block.
>
> Four legs on the left: red block at (1, 1, 2), (1, 1, 3), (1, 1, 4), (1, 1, 5).
>
> Four legs on the right: red block at (5, 1, 2), (5, 1, 3), (5, 1, 4), (5, 1, 5).
>
> Two black eyes on the front: black block at (2, 3, 3) and (4, 3, 3).

> 💡 Tip: legs stay **low** (small y). Left legs all share x = 1, right legs all share x = 5. Each leg steps **deeper** (bigger z).

Record **one video** — one take, no stopping (a phone is fine). Show these in order:

**1 · Your code.** Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

**2 · Your build.** Point the camera at the spider. Name the parts.

Say these out loud, filling in the blanks:

>
> I built it using this code: ______.
>

>
> The hardest part was ______.
>
> That part was hard because ______.
>
> The most fun part was ______.
>
> Something new I learned was ______.
