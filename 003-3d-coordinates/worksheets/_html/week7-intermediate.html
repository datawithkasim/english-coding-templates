👾 M003 Week 7 — English Worksheet (Intermediate)

Topic: Recreate 2 Mob Heads (x, y, z) · Course: 3D Coordinates · Level: Intermediate · Time: about 40 minutes

Two mob heads stand in your world — a creeper and a skeleton. Build the front face first (z stays the same), then add depth at z = 2 and z = 3.

🔴 Stand on your home spot (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your head.

1 · Predict 🔮

Read each set. Write a sentence about what you will see.

fill green block from (0, 4, 1) to (4, 8, 1)

This fills a flat wall at z = 1. Is it the front face or the depth? How many blocks deep is it so far?

fill green block from (0, 4, 1) to (4, 8, 1)
fill green block from (0, 4, 2) to (4, 8, 3)

The first line is the front face. What does the second line add? How deep is the head now?

place black block at (1, 7, 1)
place black block at (3, 7, 1)

Two eyes. Only x changes (1 → 3). Are they side by side across the front, or one behind the other?

2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

Fix each one. Then say why it was wrong.

Bug A — The head should be 3 blocks deep. Right now it is only the flat front face — paper thin.

fill green block from (0, 4, 1) to (4, 8, 1)

Hint: add the same face again at z = 2 and z = 3.

Write the fixed code:

Why was it wrong? Why does your fix work? (1–2 sentences)

Bug B — The two eyes should sit side by side on the front (z stays 1, x changes). Right now the second eye went one step deeper and hid on the side.

place black block at (1, 7, 1)
place black block at (1, 7, 2)

Hint: to move across the front, change x and keep z = 1.

Write the fixed code:

Why was it wrong? Why does your fix work? (1–2 sentences)
