🧊 M003 Extension 2 — English Worksheet (Advanced)

Topic: 3D Mob Heads (x, y, z) · Course: 3D Coordinates · Level: Advanced (Extension, after Week 3) · Time: about 45 minutes

Build two mob heads in 3D — a creeper and a piglin. Now every block needs three numbers: (x, y, z). x is across, y is up, z is forward (depth). Both heads stand in front of you in the world — walk around them, then copy one block by block.

🔴 Stand on your home spot (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your head.

1 · Predict 🔮

Read each set. Write what you will see.

place green block at (3, 5, 1)
place green block at (3, 5, 2)
place green block at (3, 5, 3)

Only z changes. Does the line go across, up, or deeper?

place green block at (4, 5, 1)
place green block at (4, 6, 1)
place green block at (4, 7, 1)

Only y changes. Across, up, or deeper?

place green block at (3, 5, 1)
place black block at (3, 5, 1)

Same (x, y, z). Green, black, or both? Why?

2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

Fix each one. Then say why it was wrong.

Bug A — This block should sit one step deeper, at (3, 5, 2). It only has two numbers, so it lands on the front.

place green block at (3, 5)

Fixed code:

Why wrong? Why does your fix work?

Bug B — The eye should be up high and one step deep: (4, 7, 2). The last two numbers are swapped.

place black block at (4, 2, 7)

Fixed code:

Why wrong? Why does your fix work?

Bug C — Code is correct, but the whole head came out one step too far forward.

(the code matched the head)

What did your friend forget before pressing run?

3 · Build a Head 🧱

Pick one head in the world. Walk around it. Then build your own on your home spot.

Start with the front face (keep z the same, e.g. z = 1). Read each block's x (across) and y (up).

Then add depth. Build the same face again at z = 2, then z = 3, until your head is as deep as the real one.

💡 Tip: the eyes and mouth only show on the front. The sides and back are plain.

Send a photo. Then finish these — 4 to 6 sentences.

I built the …

The lowest front block is at (x = …, y = …, z = …).

To go up I changed the … number; to go deeper I changed the … number.

My head is … blocks deep.

Once my head was wrong because …

Next time I would …

Film yourself showing your head. Use these words: x, y, z, across, up, forward, home spot.

  2. Walk around your head — show the front, a side, and the back.
  3. Point at one block: "this one is at x …, y …, z …".
  4. Show one block you fixed.
