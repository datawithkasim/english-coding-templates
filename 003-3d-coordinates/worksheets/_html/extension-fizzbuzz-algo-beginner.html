Topic: FizzBuzz Pixel Art (remainders & rules) · Course: 3D Coordinates · Level: Extension (Beginner, after Week 3) · Time: about 30 minutes

You already made pixel art by choosing each block's colour by hand. This time the code chooses every colour for you — using one rule, the FizzBuzz rule. Load the world, type run in the chat, and watch the agent paint a 15 × 15 wall one block at a time, counting from 1 to 225.

If the block's number… it says colour
divides by 3 and 5 FIZZ BUZZ 🟡 gold
divides by 3 FIZZ ⬛ black
divides by 5 BUZZ 🟪 purple
divides by neither (nothing) 🟩 green

🔢 Big idea: the number on a block picks its colour. Divides by 3 → black. Divides by 5 → purple. Divides by 3 and 5 → gold. Nothing → green.

2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

Bug — Block 15 divides by 3 and 5, so it should be gold. In the broken code the ÷ 3 check runs first, so block 15 turns black by mistake.

if remainder of N ÷ 3 = 0:
    paint BLACK    ← FIZZ
else if remainder of N ÷ 3 = 0 and remainder of N ÷ 5 = 0:
    paint GOLD     ← FIZZ BUZZ

What colour should block 15 really be? Circle one: gold · black

Which check should go first? Circle one: ÷ 3 · ÷ 3 and ÷ 5
