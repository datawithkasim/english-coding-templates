🗿 M003 Week 5 — English Worksheet (Intermediate)

Topic: Recreate the Statues (x, y, z) · Course: 3D Coordinates · Level: Intermediate · Time: about 40 minutes

A statue is stacked fill boxes. One fill makes a solid box between two corners. Stack the boxes higher and higher to make legs, a body, and a head. Every corner is (x, y, z) — x across, y up, z forward. To put one box on top of another, make its y start where the box below stopped.

🔴 Stand on your home spot (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your head.

1 · Predict 🔮

Read each box. Write what you will see.

fill GRAY from (3, 0, 3) to (5, 1, 5)

How wide (x) and deep (z) is this base? How tall (y) is it?

fill GRAY from (3, 2, 3) to (5, 5, 5)

This body starts at y = 2 — right on top of the base. Is it taller or shorter than the base?

fill WHITE from (3, 6, 3) to (5, 7, 5)

This box starts at y = 6. Is it near the bottom or the top of the statue?

2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

Each block is broken. Read what it should do, write the fixed code, then explain.

Bug A — The body should sit on top of the base. The base stops at y = 1, so the body should start at y = 2. Right now the body uses the same y as the base, so it lands inside it and nothing stacks up.

fill GRAY from (3, 0, 3) to (5, 1, 5)
fill GRAY from (3, 0, 3) to (5, 4, 5)

Hint: the 2nd number (y) is height. Where should the body's bottom y be?

Write the fixed code:

Why was it wrong? Why does your fix work?

Bug B — This should be the head, a box on top of the body at y = 6–7. Right now both corners use y = 6, so it is a flat slab with no height.

fill WHITE from (3, 6, 3) to (5, 6, 5)

Hint: check the 2nd column (y) in both corners.

Write the fixed code:

Why was it wrong? Why does your fix work?

3 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Walk around a statue in your world. Then build your own on your home spot. It is 3 fill boxes: a base (legs), a body, and a head.

🧱 Recipe (the numbers are (x, y, z)): - base / legs: gray from (3, 0, 3) to (5, 1, 5) - body: gray from (3, 2, 3) to (5, 5, 5) - head: white from (3, 6, 3) to (5, 7, 5)

Send a photo or video of your statue. Then finish these — 4 to 6 sentences.

First, I built the base by …

The body sits on top because its y starts at …

My statue is … tall, from y = 0 up to y = …

The number I changed to stack each box higher was y, which means …

One tricky moment was when …

If I had more time, I would …

4 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

Film yourself showing your statue. Try to use these words: fill, box, x, y, z, stack, home spot.

  1. Show your home spot and stand on it. Say why you stand there every run.
  2. Point at the base, the body, and the head. Read one corner of each out loud.
  3. Show two boxes and say which one sits higher, and which number makes it higher.
  4. Say in your own words what each of x, y, and z does.
