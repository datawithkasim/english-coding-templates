Open the world. One burger is finished. One is only started — it has a bottom bun and a tomato, nothing else.

Your job: finish it. Every block sits at (x, y) — x is how far across, y is how far up.

🔴 Put a red block at your feet before you start. That is your home spot. Stand on it every run, or the burger moves too.

1 · Predict 🔮

Read the steps. Circle your answer.

place red block at (2, 4)
place red block at (3, 4)
place red block at (4, 4)

A line across or up? Circle one: across · up

Which number stays the same? Circle one: x · y

place brown block at (8, 9)
place brown block at (8, 10)

Across or up? Circle one: across · up

Which number changes? Circle one: x · y

2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

This code is broken. Read what it should do, then fill in the fix.

Bug — The yellow cheese belongs above the lettuce, at y = 8. This block lands on the tomato at y = 4.

place yellow block at (2, 4)

Hint: y is up. A bigger y is higher on the wall.

Fill the blank to fix the line:

place yellow block at (2, __)

Which number fills the blank? Circle one: 4 · 8

Why was the code wrong? Circle one: the y was too low · the colour was wrong

3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Stand on your home spot (the red block).

Warm up first. Build one row — the cheese. It is yellow, at y = 8, from x = 2 to x = 14.

Now finish the burger. Copy every missing row from the picture, reading each square's (x, y).
