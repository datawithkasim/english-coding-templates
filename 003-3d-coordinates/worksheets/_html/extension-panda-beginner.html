🐼 M003 Extension — Build the Panda (Beginner)

Topic: 3D Panda (x, y, z) · Course: 3D Coordinates · Level: Beginner (Extension, after Week 6) · Time: about 30 minutes

Build a standing panda in 3D — white body, black ears, black eyes, black legs. Every block needs three numbers (x, y, z): x across, y up, z deeper (forward).

🔴 Stand on your home spot (red block) every run. Move, and the panda moves too.

🎨 White parts = white concrete. Black parts = black wool.

1 · Predict 🔮

Read the code. Circle one word.

place black block at (2, 5, 1)
place black block at (2, 6, 1)
place black block at (2, 7, 1)

Only y changes. This is a leg climbing... Circle one: across · up · deeper

place white block at (2, 8, 1)
place white block at (2, 8, 2)
place white block at (2, 8, 3)

Only z changes. Circle one: across · up · deeper

2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

An ear sits on top of the head, one step up, at (2, 9, 1). The code below has only two numbers, so it lands flat on the ground.

# broken
place black block at (2, 9)

Fill the blank to fix it:

place black block at (2, 9, __)

Which number fills the blank? Circle one: 1 · 3

What was missing? Circle one: the z number · the color

3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Look at the 🐼 panda in the world. Walk around it. See the ears up top, the eye patches, and the two legs at the bottom.

Build a small panda on your home spot. Keep it easy:

White head: fill white blocks from (2, 5, 1) up to (5, 8, 1) — a small square.

Two black ears on top: black block at (2, 9, 1) and (5, 9, 1).

Two black eyes on the front: black block at (2, 7, 1) and (5, 7, 1).

Two black legs at the bottom: black block at (2, 4, 1) and (5, 4, 1).

💡 Tip: ears go up (bigger y). Legs go down (smaller y). Eyes only show on the front (z = 1).

Record one video — one take, no stopping (a phone is fine). Show these in order:

1 · Your code. Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

2 · Your build. Point the camera at the panda. Name the parts.

Say these out loud, filling in the blanks:
