🧊 M003 Extension 2 — English Worksheet (Beginner)

Topic: 3D Mob Heads (x, y, z) · Course: 3D Coordinates · Level: Extension (Beginner, after Week 3) · Time: about 30 minutes

Build a mob head in 3D. Now every block needs three numbers: (x, y, z). x is across, y is up, z is forward (depth). The heads stand in front of you in the world — look at one as you build.

🔴 Stand on your home spot (red block) every run. Move, and the head moves too.

1 · Predict 🔮

Read the steps. Circle or write your answer.

place green block at (3, 5, 1)
place green block at (3, 5, 2)
place green block at (3, 5, 3)

Only z changes. Circle one: across · up · deeper

place green block at (4, 5, 1)
place green block at (4, 6, 1)

Only y changes. Circle one: across · up · deeper

2 · Find the Difference 🐛

Correct first, then broken. Write one sentence about the bug.

Pair A — This block should be one step deep: (3, 5, 2).

# correct
place green block at (3, 5, 2)

# broken
place green block at (3, 5, 1)

What is wrong? Which number is different?

Pair B — The eye should be at (4, 7, 2).

# correct
place black block at (4, 7, 2)

# broken
place black block at (4, 2, 7)

What is wrong? Which two numbers got swapped?

3 · Fill the Gap ✏️

To go deeper, the third number (z) grows. Fill the blanks to go deeper at (3, 5).

place green block at (3, 5, 1)
place green block at (3, 5, __)
place green block at (3, 5, __)

4 · Build a Head 🧱

Look at the 🟢 creeper head in the world. Walk around it.

Build the front face first (keep z = 1). Read each block's x (across) and y (up).

Then add depth — build the same face again at z = 2, then z = 3.

💡 Tip: the eyes and mouth only show on the front. The sides and back are plain.

Send a photo. Then finish these — 3 to 5 sentences.

I built the …

The lowest front block is at (x = …, y = …, z = …).

To go up I changed the … number; to go deeper I changed the … number.

My head is … blocks deep.

Next time I would …
