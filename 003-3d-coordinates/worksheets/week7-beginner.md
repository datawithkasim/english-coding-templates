# 👾 M003 Week 7 — English Worksheet (Beginner)

**Topic:** Recreate 2 Mob Heads (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Beginner · **Time:** about 30 minutes

Two blocky **mob heads** stand in your world — each a cube with a face on the **front**. Every block uses **(x, y, z)** — x **across**, y **up**, z **deep**.

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your head.

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read the code. Circle one word.

```
place green block at (0, 4, 1)
place green block at (0, 4, 2)
```

**Only z changes (1 → 2). Circle one:** across · up · deeper

```
place green block at (0, 4, 1)
place green block at (0, 5, 1)
```

**Only y changes (4 → 5). Circle one:** across · up · deeper

---

## 2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

An eye should be on the **front** at z = 1 — the face only shows there.

```
# broken
place black block at (2, 5, 2)
```

**Fill the blank to put the eye back on the front:**

```
place black block at (2, 5, __)
```

**Which number fills the blank? Circle one:** 1 · 2

**Why was it wrong? Circle one:** z was 2, so the eye went to the side · the color was wrong

---

## 3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Build this small green cube head and add **two eyes** on the front. Stand on your home spot first.

```
fill green block from (0, 4, 1) to (2, 6, 2)
place black block at (0, 5, 1)
place black block at (2, 5, 1)
```

This is a **3×3 front, 2 blocks deep**, with two eyes on the front.

Record **one video** (a phone is fine). Show two things:

**1 · Your code.** Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

**2 · Your build.** Point the camera. Name the parts.

Say these out loud, filling in the blanks:

> Today I built ______.
>
> I built it using this code: ______.
>
> In this code I used ______.
>
> The hardest part was ______.
>
> That part was hard because ______.
