# ☃️ M003 Extension — Build the Snow Golem (Intermediate)

**Topic:** 3D Snow Golem (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Intermediate (Extension, after Week 6) · **Time:** about 40 minutes

Build a **standing snow golem** in 3D — a snow body, a carved-pumpkin head, coal buttons down the front, and two stick arms. Every block needs **three** numbers **(x, y, z)**: x **across**, y **up**, z **deeper** (forward).

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move your feet, move your golem.
>
> 🎨 Snow = **snow block**. Head = **carved pumpkin**. Buttons + eyes = **coal block**. Arms = a **brown block** (oak log).

## 3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Walk around the ☃️ snow golem in the world. Then build a snow golem on your home spot.

> **Snow body:** fill snow blocks from (2, 1, 1) to (4, 5, 1) — a tall tower.
>
> **Pumpkin head on top** (bigger y): fill carved pumpkin from (2, 6, 1) to (4, 7, 1).
>
> **Two coal eyes on the head** (front): coal block at (2, 7, 1) and (4, 7, 1).
>
> **Two coal buttons on the body** (front): coal block at (3, 4, 1) and (3, 2, 1).
>
> **Two arms out the sides:** a brown block at (1, 4, 1) and (5, 4, 1).
>
> **Add depth:** build the snow body and pumpkin head again at z = 2. The eyes, buttons, and arms stay on the **front only** (z = 1).

> 💡 Tip: the head goes **up** (bigger y). The arms poke **out** (x = 1 and x = 5). A bigger z means **deeper** into the wall.

Record **one video** — one take, no stopping (a phone is fine). Show these in order:

**1 · Your code.** Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

**2 · Your build.** Point the camera at the golem. Name the parts.
