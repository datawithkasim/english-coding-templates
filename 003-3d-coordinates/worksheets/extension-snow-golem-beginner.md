# ☃️ M003 Extension — Build the Snow Golem (Beginner)

**Topic:** 3D Snow Golem (x, y, z) · **Course:** 3D Coordinates · **Level:** Beginner (Extension, after Week 6) · **Time:** about 30 minutes

Build a standing snow golem in 3D — **snow body**, **pumpkin head**, **coal buttons**, **stick arms**. Every block needs **three** numbers **(x, y, z)**: x **across**, y **up**, z **deeper** (forward).

> 🔴 Stand on your **home spot** (red block) every run. Move, and the golem moves too.
>
> 🎨 Snow = **snow block**. Head = **carved pumpkin**. Buttons + eyes = **coal block**. Arms = a **brown block** (oak log).

---

## 1 · Predict 🔮

Read the code. Circle one word.

```
place snow block at (3, 1, 1)
place snow block at (3, 2, 1)
place snow block at (3, 3, 1)
```

**Only y changes. This snow tower is going... Circle one:** across · up · deeper

```
place snow block at (3, 3, 1)
place snow block at (3, 3, 2)
place snow block at (3, 3, 3)
```

**Only z changes. Circle one:** across · up · deeper

---

## 2 · Spot the Bug 🐛

A coal button sits **on the front** of the body at (3, 4, 1). The code below has only **two** numbers, so it lands flat on the ground.

```
# broken
place coal block at (3, 4)
```

**Fill the blank to fix it:**

```
place coal block at (3, 4, __)
```

**Which number fills the blank? Circle one:** 1 · 3

**What was missing? Circle one:** the z number · the color

---

## 3 · Show Your Work 📸🎥

Look at the ☃️ snow golem in the world. Walk around it. See the pumpkin head up top, the coal buttons down the front, and the two arms poking out the sides.

**Build a small snow golem on your home spot.** Keep it easy:

> **Snow body:** fill snow blocks from (2, 1, 1) up to (4, 5, 1) — a tall tower.
>
> **Pumpkin head on top:** fill carved pumpkin from (2, 6, 1) to (4, 7, 1).
>
> **Two coal eyes on the head:** coal block at (2, 7, 1) and (4, 7, 1).
>
> **Two coal buttons on the body:** coal block at (3, 4, 1) and (3, 2, 1).
>
> **Two arms out the sides:** a brown block at (1, 4, 1) and (5, 4, 1).

> 💡 Tip: the head goes **up** (bigger y). The arms poke **out** (x = 1 and x = 5). Everything shows on the **front** (z = 1).

Record **one video** — one take, no stopping (a phone is fine). Show these in order:

**1 · Your code.** Scroll through it. Say what each part does.

**2 · Your build.** Point the camera at the golem. Name the parts.
