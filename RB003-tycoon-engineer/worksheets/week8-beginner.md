## 4 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Now publish your tycoon: give it a **name** and an **icon**, and set it to **Public**. Then play your published game — earn cash, leave, rejoin, and check your cash is still there.

Send a photo or video of your game's page, then explain. Use these sentence starters — write 2 or 3 sentences.

> My game is called …
>
> My favorite part is …
>
> The hardest bug was …

## 5 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

Take a video on your phone (or a parent's phone) — this is your **presentation**. Talk like you are showing your game to a new player. Try to use these words: **publish**, **public**, **upgrade**.

> 1. Show your game's page — its name and icon.
> 2. Play: earn cash and buy one upgrade.
> 3. Leave and rejoin — show your cash is still there.

**Write what you will say in your video.** You can read from it while filming.
