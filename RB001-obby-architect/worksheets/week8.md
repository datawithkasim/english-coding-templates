## 3 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Now publish your obby: **File → Publish to Roblox**, write a real name and a 1–2 sentence description, turn on **Public**, and add an icon. Open your game's page on the Roblox website. When you finish, come back here.

Send a photo or video of your game's public page, then tell the story of your game. Use these sentence starters — write 4 to 6 sentences total.

> My game is called … and in it you …
>
> The hardest bug I fixed was …
>
> I fixed it by …
>
> My favorite script is … because it …
>
> If I kept building, the next thing I would add is …
