## 2 · Find the Difference 🐛

Each pair shows a clean checklist first, then a broken one. Circle what's different and write one short sentence about the problem.

**Pair A** — Friends should be able to find and play the game.

```
-- clean
Game name:    Super Lava Run
Description:  Jump over lava and win!
Public:       ON
```

```
-- buggy
Game name:    Super Lava Run
Description:  Jump over lava and win!
Public:       OFF (Private)
```

**What is wrong? What does your friend see?**

## 4 · Tell Me What You Built 📸

Now publish your obby: **File → Publish to Roblox**, write a name and a short description, and turn on **Public**. Open your game's page on the Roblox website. When you finish, come back here.

Send a photo or video of your game's public page, then tell us about your game. Use these sentence starters — write 2 or 3 sentences.

> My game is called … and in it you …
>
> The hardest bug I fixed was …
>
> Next, I would build …

## 5 · Record Your Walkthrough 🎥

This is your **presentation video**. Take a video on your phone (or a parent's phone) and present your published game. Try to use these words: **publish**, **public**, **bug**.

> 1. Show your game's page and say its name.
> 2. Play through one section of your obby.
> 3. Tell one bug you fixed and how you fixed it.

**Write what you will say in your video.** You can read from it while filming.
